# Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

I believe that Focus Bear was created for the pure intention of helping people who struggle with neurodivergent tendencies, as that of ADHD,, in navigating through daily routines productively, punctually, and peacefully. This app is here to help fight off distractions and foster a productive lifestyle.

# What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Focus Bear is actively solving the issue of distraction, unproductivity, and lack of focus especially when using you device such as smartphones and computers.

# Why do you think this mission is important?

Many people struggle in keeping themselves in line with the task at hand. Due to distractions and other factors of unproductivity, completing these tasks serve to be bothersome and difficult than they should be, and even at times cannot be completed at all. So with Focus Bear and its mission, solving these issues does not only help people complete tasks alone, but also improve their quality of life.
