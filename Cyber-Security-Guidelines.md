# Reflection

### What security measures do you currently follow, and where can you improve?

I primarily use complex passwords for my accounts and always make sure that my desktop and laptop are secured with updated security. I can improve by implementing a password manager, since I noticed that I tend to have several retries on certain accounts due to the varying complexity of the passwords.

### How can you make secure behaviour a habit rather than an afterthought?

Whenever creating a new account, the first thing that is good to check is if they have 2FA features or other security features in their settings. Every now and then it is best make sure that your accounts are still secured, and possibly use a VPN every now and then if you really want your digital footprint to be safer for yourself especially when handling sensitive information (like coding for Focus Bear).

### What steps will you take to ensure your passwords and accounts are secure?

Always make sure that your passwords are complex with differing letters, numbers, and symbols. If there is a 2FA option for your account, it is best to enable it and make sure that every account you have will more or less have differing passwords.

### What would you do if you suspected a security breach or suspicious activity on your account?

I would immediately log out of those accounts and change the password. If it's within the company, I would also notify a higher up or security personnel about the suspicious activity.
