# Reflection

### What do you think are the biggest benefits and challenges of Agile?

The biggest benefit is speed to market. As a Frontend Developer, I can see my UI changes live and helping users almost immediately. The biggest challenge is constant change. It requires high discipline to stay focused on the current task while priorities shift around you, which is why using Focus Bear is so helpful for our team.

### How can Agile principles help improve your role and responsibilities?

Agile principles encourage frequent feedback. Instead of spending a week building a complex UI component only to find out it doesn't meet the PM's vision, I can share a "Work in Progress" early. This saves time and ensures my technical implementation is always aligned with the user's needs.

# Task

### Summarize the main differences between Scrum and Kanban in your own words.

Scrum is like a relay race run in short, timed heats. Everyone knows exactly what they are doing for that 2-week heat. Kanban is like a continuous stream. We pull the next most important task from the backlog as soon as we have the capacity to work on it, keeping the development flow constant without the pressure of artificial sprint deadlines.

### Identify one Agile principle you think will be most useful in your work.

Our highest priority is to satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software. As a Frontend Intern, this principle reminds me that a simple, working UI delivered today is often more valuable to our users than a "perfect" one delivered a month from now.
