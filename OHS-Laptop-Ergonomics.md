# What equipment changes can you make to improve your workspace setup? (e.g., using an external monitor, adjusting your chair, using a laptop stand)

I purchased a wrist rest for both keyboard and mouse to ergonomically position my wrists to have less strain.

# What behavioural changes can you implement to improve posture and reduce strain? (e.g., sitting upright, taking regular breaks, adjusting screen height)

I run a two-monitor setup but they were positioned side by side. Since this was straining on my neck, I decided to stack my monitors instead. I placed one monitor on top of the other and it proved to be more ergonomic for my eye view. Instead of moving my neck left and right to switch between monitors, I just have to move my eyes to look up and down.

# How can you remind yourself to maintain good posture and take breaks throughout the day? (Hint: Use Focus Bear to schedule movement breaks!)

After finishing a certain task, that's when I usually take breaks. This can further be improved now that I am using Focus Bear and the Focus Session feature to remind me to take breaks using the Pomodoro method.
