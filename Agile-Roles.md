# Reflection

### What is your role, and how does it contribute to the Agile process?

My role is a Frontend Developer Intern. I contribute to the Agile process by translating UI/UX designs into functional code using frameworks like React. By moving frontend tasks through the Kanban board, I ensure that the user-facing side of the application is constantly improving and that stakeholders can see visual progress in every development cycle.

### How do your responsibilities overlap or interact with other roles in the team?

My role sits at the intersection of UX Design and Backend Development. I interact with designers to ensure technical feasibility of their mockups and with backend developers to define how data will be fetched via APIs. I also overlap with QA to ensure that my components meet accessibility standards and work seamlessly.

# Task

### Write a short description of how your role fits into the Agile team.

As a Frontend Developer Intern, I am responsible for building the visual and interactive elements of Focus Bear. I fit into the Agile team by maintaining UI development, providing technical feedback on user stories, and ensuring that my work aligns with the overall product vision shared in our daily stand-ups.

### Identify one other role you frequently collaborate with and describe how you can improve teamwork with them.

I frequently collaborate with UX Designers. I can improve teamwork by participating in the design phase earlier to discuss "technical constraints." By helping designers understand what is easily implementable versus what might take longer to build, we can streamline the development process and avoid mid-sprint design changes.
