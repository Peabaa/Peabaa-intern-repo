# Reflection

### How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?

Agile ceremonies provide a structured, predictable rhythm for communication. Instead of needing constant unscheduled meetings that interrupt deep work, ceremonies like the Daily Stand-up ensure that everyone is aligned on priorities and that blockers are identified early. It creates a "single source of truth" for the project's progress.

### Which ceremony do you think is most important for your role, and why?

As an intern, I believe Daily Stand-ups are the most important. Because I am still learning the codebase, Stand-ups ensure that if I am stuck on a technical issue, I can flag it immediately. This prevents me from wasting hours on a problem that a senior developer could help me solve in five minutes, keeping my internship productive.

# Task

### Document one change you can make to improve your team collaboration.

I will adopt the habit of "Aggressive Documentation" on my GitHub Pull Requests. Instead of just pushing code, I will provide a clear summary of what I changed and why. This supports the team's asynchronous workflow by allowing reviewers to understand my logic without needing a live meeting to explain it.
